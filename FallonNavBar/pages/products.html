About us

We are a small, locally-owned cafe, located on 4233 Ridge Ave in Philadelphia, PA. Our mission is to serve the highest quality coffee in an inviting and relaxed atmosphere. We strive to make each and every customer feel welcome and at home. We offer a wide selection of specialty coffees, teas, and pastries, with something to please everyone. Our baristas have years of experience and take pride in delivering the perfect cup of coffee every time. Stop in for your morning cup or an afternoon pick-me-up. We look forward to serving you soon!
